 Hi, I am Bhim Sen Mishra. I am a professional E-commerce Project Manager with more than 2 years of experience in planning, designing, developing and managing IT projects. My inclination towards programming started in 10th grade when I got familiar with HTML as a part of my curriculum. Continuing this I choose Computer Science with Mathematics and Physics as my majors in High school. There I was introduced with basic programming languages C and C++.

Continuing education I graduated with a Bachelor’s of Computer Science, which inculcated in me advance programming skills including other languages like Java, C#, PHP, Ruby, SQL and PL/SQL and confronted me with its radical practical applications.

After my graduation, I joined “JK Enterprise” as a “System Analyst”, where I worked on SaaS projects and in improving the technical environment of the company.

In my quest for education that would add more professional skills and also will provide global exposure, I clicked into Project Management IT graduate certificate program in Seneca where I learned about various project development methodologies. In my second semester, I landed to a startup “American Tall” as “Project Coordinator Intern” and helped them build their web store using Shopify.

I also have a Diploma in Financial Services and International Business Management which have deepened my knowledge of developing professional business plan, finance and accounting principles with Canadian legal framework.

Apart from this, I have received acclamation for my research papers on “Golden Ratio” and “Partial Derivatives”. Recently I have received “Award of Excellence 2017” from “Seneca College” for winning “HRSA Business Case Competition” drafted by Ivey and ISB business schools.  

Thanks for patiently reading this post and considering my efforts worth noticing. I'd love to hear from you.
